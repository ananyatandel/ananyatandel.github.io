ABOUT ME

Ananya Tandel

Hello & welcome! My name is Ananya (pronounced uh-nuhn yuh) and my pronouns are she/her/hers. I am an Undergraduate Sophomore at University of Colorado Boulder majoring in Creative Technology & Design and minoring in Computer Science and Business. At CU Boulder, I am the Co-Events Chair for South Asian Student Association (also known as SASA), a Journey Leader, an Intramural Badminton Player, and a member of CU Women In Computing. My main goals for this ATLS 2200 (Web) are to enhance my web development skills and make websites visually pleasing and functional for everyone.


Here are some fun facts about me:

  1. I was born and raised in India and moved to Westminster when I turned nine years old.
  2. I am a huge fan of Harry Styles and Taylor Swift!
  3. I've been playing both tennis and badminton since I was five.
  4. My favorite book is The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo by Taylor Jenkins Reid!
  5. I am quadrilingual. (Fluent in English, Gujarati, Hindi, and Spanish!)
