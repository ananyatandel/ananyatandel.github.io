ABOUT ME

Ananya Tandel

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Ananya (pronounced uh-nuhn yuh) and my pronouns are she/her/hers. I am an Undergraduate Sophomore at University of Colorado Boulder (also known as CU Boulder) pursuing a B.S. in Creative Technology & Design and minoring in Computer Science and Business. At CU Boulder, I am the Co-Events Chair for South Asian Student Association (SASA), a Journey Leader, an Intramural Badminton Player, and a member of CU Women In Computing. This website takes you through my projects, résumé, and contact information! This was initially created as a midterm project for my Web course at CU Boulder. My main goals for this class are to enhance my web development skills and make websites visually pleasing and functional for everyone. A little bit more about me: In my free time, you can find me at the courts playing tennis or badminton; I consider myself an avid reader, traveler, and concert enthusiast!


Here are some more fun facts about me:

  1. I was born and raised in India and moved to Westminster, Colorado when I turned nine years old.
  2. I am a huge fan of Harry Styles and Taylor Swift!
  3. I've been playing both tennis and badminton since I was five.
  4. My favorite book is The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo by Taylor Jenkins Reid!
  5. I am quadrilingual. (Fluent in English, Gujarati, Hindi, and Spanish!)
